M&M Omemee — One Stop Shop in Omemee

Your Local One Stop Shop

M&M Omemee is a small, locally owned shop proudly serving the Omemee community since 2019.

We’re not a big box store — we’re the kind of place where people stop in for a coffee, ask a quick question, pick up what they need, and leave with a smile.

What You’ll Find Here

Coffee Time for your daily coffee and treats, an Authorized M&M Food Market Express selection for busy days, plus hardware essentials, beer, and lottery — all in one stop.

It’s not about having everything — it’s about having what people actually need.
